# What is Thermal calculation amd Why we are doing it?
In schematic/PCB design, thermal calculation means analysing how much heat each component generates and how that heat will be dissipated through the PCB and surrounding environment.
It helps ensure the board does not overheat and that each component stays within its safe operating temperature.

So product in  Automotive or aero space have some criteria to pass the test in different environment and other test like EMI/EMC
